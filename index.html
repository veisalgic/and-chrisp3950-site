Our Story

A Community Bakery, Through & Through

Miller's Bakery has been serving the Johnson City community since 2018. What started as a passion for honest, handcrafted baking has grown into a beloved neighborhood gathering spot where familiar faces and fresh aromas greet you every morning.

Our small, dedicated team shows up before sunrise to make sure every loaf, pastry, and celebration cake is made entirely from scratch. We believe great baking takes time, care, and a whole lot of heart — and that's exactly what we put into everything that comes out of our oven.
