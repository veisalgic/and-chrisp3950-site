Our Story

A Community Bakery Born from Scratch

Miller's Bakery has been a beloved part of the Johnson City community since 2018. We started with a simple belief: that real, handcrafted baked goods made with quality ingredients and genuine care can bring people together.

Our small, passionate team shows up before dawn every single day to make sure that when you walk through our door on Main Street, everything coming out of the oven is as fresh as it gets. From your morning sourdough to your daughter's birthday cake, we put love into every single thing we make.

What We Make

Our Specialties

Every item on our menu is made from scratch each morning — because you deserve better than day-old anything.

🎂

Custom Celebration Cakes

From elegant wedding cakes to fun birthday creations, our custom cakes are designed and baked just for you. We work with you to bring your vision to life — just place your order 48 hours in advance.

🥐

Seasonal Pastries

Flaky croissants, fruit danishes, cinnamon rolls, and rotating seasonal treats made with the freshest ingredients of the moment. Follow us on social media to see what's fresh this week.

🍞

Fresh Sourdough Breads

Our sourdough is made from a living starter and baked fresh every single morning. With a crispy crust and chewy, tangy crumb — it's the real deal. Come early; it sells out fast.

📋

Special Orders Welcome

Need something extra special? We offer a full range of special-order items — custom cakes, large cookie trays for the office, pastry platters for events, and more. All we need is 48 hours notice and we'll take great care of you.
