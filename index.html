Our Story

Where Every Scoop Tells a Story

Apple Creek Creamery was born in the spring of 2011 when Tom and Linda Miller decided to turn their lifelong passion for farm-fresh dairy into something the whole community could enjoy. Starting with just two flavors and a hand-painted roadside sign, they opened their doors on a warm May afternoon — and by closing time, they'd sold out completely. That first day told them everything they needed to know.

Over the past decade-plus, the Millers have grown their small-batch operation into a beloved seasonal destination, drawing visitors from as far as Columbus and Cleveland who make the drive to Apple Creek just for a cone. Son Derek joined the family business in 2018, bringing with him a culinary degree and a head full of adventurous flavor ideas that have since become some of the shop's most celebrated offerings.

Our mission has never wavered: use the best ingredients we can source, make everything in small batches with care, and serve every single customer like a neighbor. We partner with Amish dairy farms just down the road for our cream and milk, ensuring every scoop you eat is tied to this land, this community, and these people we're proud to call friends.

We believe ice cream is more than a dessert — it's a moment. It's a summer evening, a first date, a grandchild's birthday. We take that seriously, which is why we never cut corners, never rush a batch, and never stop experimenting with new ways to make your day a little sweeter. Apple Creek Creamery is family-owned, community-rooted, and deeply grateful for every customer who walks through our door.
