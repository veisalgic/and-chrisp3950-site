Our Story

A Community Bakery, Through and Through

Miller's Bakery has been a beloved part of the Johnson City community since 2018. Tucked away at 142 Main Street, our cozy storefront is a gathering place for neighbors, families, and anyone who believes that a really good pastry can make any day better.

Our small, dedicated team arrives before dawn every single day to hand-craft everything from scratch — because you deserve better than a factory loaf. We put real care and real ingredients into every item that comes out of our oven, from a simple snickerdoodle to a five-tier wedding cake.

  • Made from scratch every morning, no exceptions
  • Custom celebration & wedding cakes available
  • Seasonal pastries that change with the harvest
  • Small team, big heart — we know our regulars by name
  • Special orders ready in 48 hours with advance notice

What We Bake

Something Delicious for Every Occasion

Whether you're swinging by for your morning coffee companion or planning the cake of a lifetime, we've got you covered from Tuesday through Sunday.

🍞

Sourdough Breads

Classic, naturally leavened sourdough baked fresh every single morning. Crusty on the outside, tender within — perfect for toast, sandwiches, or simply tearing apart at the table.

🎂

Celebration Cakes

From birthday smash cakes to elegant multi-tier wedding creations, our custom cakes are designed with your vision in mind. Just reach out at least 48 hours in advance to start planning.

🥐

Pastries & Croissants

Buttery, flaky, and absolutely worth the detour. Our rotating pastry case includes croissants, danishes, scones, and sweet rolls — all made in small batches so they're always fresh.

🍪

Cookies & Treats

A dozen cookies for the office? A box of brownies for a thank-you gift? We've got a full lineup of classic and seasonal cookies that are dangerously good and beautifully packaged.

🌸

Seasonal Specialties

We follow the seasons, not a frozen catalog. Expect pumpkin spice favorites in fall, fruit galettes in summer, and festive holiday collections that sell out fast. Follow us online so you never miss a drop.

📦

Special Orders

Need something specific? Just call ahead or stop by the shop. With 48 hours notice, we can create almost anything your heart desires — from allergy-friendly options to custom-flavored creations.

📅 Planning Something Special?

Give us at least 48 hours notice and we'll take care of the rest. Stop by 142 Main Street or give us a call — we love making your events sweeter.
