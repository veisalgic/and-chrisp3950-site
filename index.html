Our Story

Born From a Love of Simple, Sweet Moments

Apple Creek Creamery was born from a simple dream: to create a place in Jonesborough where neighbors could slow down, share a scoop, and feel genuinely welcome. Founded by the Miller family — Chris, Sarah, and their two kids — the creamery opened its doors on a warm spring Saturday at their home on Forest Circle. What started as a backyard ice cream churn tradition quickly grew into something the whole community wanted to be part of.

Every recipe was developed right here in Tennessee, inspired by the flavors of Appalachian life — crisp orchard apples, wild blackberries, honeysuckle, and fresh peaches from nearby farms. Sarah spent years refining the base recipes until they hit that perfect balance of richness and freshness. Chris handled the creative flavors, dreaming up new combinations each season and testing them on their most honest critics: the neighborhood kids.

Our mission has always been more than ice cream. We wanted to build a gathering place — a spot where people linger on a bench under the trees, where laughter floats on a summer breeze, and where a $4 scoop can make a hard day feel a little lighter.

We rotate our flavors with the seasons because we believe food should reflect the world around us. Follow us on Facebook to see what's scooping this week — we love sharing the news and hearing your reactions when a new flavor drops. Thank you, Jonesborough, for making us your sweet escape.
