Our Story

A Family Passion, Turned Community Staple

Since opening our doors in 2018, Miller's Bakery has grown from a small dream into one of Johnson City's most beloved spots. Every loaf, every croissant, and every celebration cake is made from scratch by our small, dedicated team — the same way it's always been done.


We believe great baking starts with great ingredients and a genuine love for what you do. Whether you stop in for your morning coffee and a pastry or you're trusting us with your wedding cake, we treat every order like it's the most important thing coming out of our oven — because it is.

Made From Scratch, Every Single Day

Our bakers arrive before sunrise to mix, proof, and bake so that when you walk through our door, everything is as fresh as it gets. No shortcuts, no preservatives — just honest, wholesome baking the way it should be.
