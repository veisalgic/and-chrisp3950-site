Our Story

A Little Bakery
with a Big Heart

Miller's Bakery has been a beloved part of the Johnson City community since we first opened our doors in 2018. What started as a passion for wholesome, handcrafted baking has grown into a cozy neighborhood staple where familiar faces come for their morning bread and families return for every milestone cake.

Our small, dedicated team arrives before the sun is up — mixing, proofing, and shaping everything from scratch so that when you walk through the door, everything is at its absolute freshest.
