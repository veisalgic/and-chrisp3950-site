Our Story

A Little Bakery with a Big Heart

Miller's Bakery has been a cherished part of the Johnson City community since 2018. What started as a passion for honest, from-scratch baking has grown into a neighborhood institution — a place where locals stop in for their morning sourdough and return to celebrate life's biggest moments.

Our small, dedicated team starts each day before sunrise, mixing doughs, folding pastries, and decorating custom cakes so that everything is at its freshest when you walk through the door.
