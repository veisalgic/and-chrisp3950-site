Our Story

A Community Bakery You Can Count On

Miller's Bakery has been serving fresh, handcrafted baked goods to the Johnson City community since 2018. What started as a passion for scratch-made treats has grown into a beloved neighborhood staple.

Our small, dedicated team shows up before sunrise every day to bake from scratch — so when you walk through our doors, everything is as fresh as it gets. From the first loaf of sourdough pulled from the oven to the last celebration cake decorated with care, every item reflects our commitment to quality and community.

We're more than a bakery — we're a place where neighbors gather, milestones get celebrated, and every visit feels like home.
