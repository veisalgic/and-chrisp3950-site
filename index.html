Our Story

A Little Bakery with a Big Heart

Miller's Bakery has been a cornerstone of the Johnson City community since we first opened our doors in 2018. What started as a passion project quickly became a beloved gathering place — because when something is made with real care, people can taste the difference.

Our small, dedicated team arrives before sunrise every morning to shape, proof, and bake everything fresh from scratch. No shortcuts, no preservatives — just quality ingredients, time-honored techniques, and a whole lot of heart poured into every batch.

Find us at 142 Main Street, where the smell of fresh bread and warm pastries will greet you at the door. We can't wait to welcome you in.

What We Make

Fresh From Our Oven

Everything on our menu is crafted in small batches using quality ingredients. Here's a look at what you'll find when you visit us.

🎂

Custom Celebration Cakes

From birthday smashes to elegant wedding tiers, we design and bake custom cakes for every milestone. Tell us your vision and we'll bring it to life — beautifully and deliciously.

🍞

Fresh Sourdough Breads

Our signature sourdough is slow-fermented and baked fresh every morning. With a perfectly crackling crust and tender, tangy crumb, it's worth setting your alarm a little earlier for.

🥐

Seasonal Pastries

Buttery croissants, fruit danishes, morning buns, and rotating seasonal creations that celebrate the best flavors of each time of year. Follow us on social to see what's fresh this week.

🍪

Cookies & Sweet Treats

Need a dozen cookies for the office? A box of brownies for a thank-you gift? Our rotating selection of scratch-made cookies and bars are crowd-pleasers every single time.

🫐

Muffins & Quick Breads

Blueberry, lemon-poppy, banana walnut — our muffins and quick breads make the perfect breakfast or afternoon snack. Fresh every morning until they're gone!
