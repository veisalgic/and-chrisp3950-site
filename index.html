Our Story

Baked from Scratch,
Every Single Day

Miller's Bakery has been a beloved part of the Johnson City community since we first opened our doors in 2018. What started as a passion for real, wholesome baking has grown into a neighborhood institution — a cozy spot where the smell of fresh bread and warm pastries greets you before you even walk in.

Our small, dedicated team shows up early every morning to mix, proof, and bake everything from scratch. No shortcuts, no compromises. Just honest, handcrafted food made with ingredients you can trust — and a whole lot of heart.
