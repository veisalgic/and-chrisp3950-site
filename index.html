Our Story

A Small Team with a Big Oven Full of Love

Miller's Bakery opened its doors in 2018 with one simple mission: bring the warmth and care of scratch-made baking back to the Johnson City community. Every loaf, every cookie, and every cake is made by hand — no shortcuts, no mixes, no compromises.

Our cozy storefront at 142 Main Street is where neighbors become regulars and regulars become family. Whether you're grabbing a morning sourdough on your way to work or picking up a dozen cookies for the office, you'll always find something fresh coming out of the oven.

What We Make

Custom Celebration Cakes

Weddings, birthdays, anniversaries — we create showstopping cakes tailored to your vision. Just give us 48 hours notice and we'll make the moment extra special.

Fresh Sourdough Breads

Our sourdough is made from scratch every morning using a long-fermented starter. Crusty on the outside, pillowy on the inside, and worth waking up early for.

🥐

Seasonal Pastries

From buttery croissants to fruit-filled danishes, our pastry case changes with the seasons — always showcasing the best flavors the time of year has to offer.

Cookies & Bars
