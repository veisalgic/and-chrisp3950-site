Our Story

Baked with Tradition,
Served with Heart

Miller's Bakery was founded with one simple belief: great baked goods start with great ingredients and a whole lot of care. What began as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery serving our community daily.

Our bakers arrive before dawn every morning to ensure that every sourdough, croissant, and birthday cake that leaves our shop is fresh, warm, and made to perfection. We source our flour, eggs, and dairy from trusted local farms just miles away.

Whether you're stopping in for your morning coffee and a muffin or picking up a custom celebration cake, you're always welcome at Miller's.

Artisan Breads

Pastries & Croissants

Buttery, flaky croissants, pain au chocolat, almond danish, and fresh cinnamon rolls glazed to golden perfection.
