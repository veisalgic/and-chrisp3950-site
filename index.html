Our Story

A Neighborhood Bakery Built on Tradition

Miller's Bakery started as a small family kitchen and grew into the neighborhood's most beloved gathering spot. We believe that great bread is a form of love — and every loaf we bake carries that belief.

We source our flour from local mills, use seasonal fruit, and never take shortcuts. From our signature sourdough to our flaky croissants, everything is made fresh each morning before the sun rises.
