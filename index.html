Our Story

A Family Bakery, Rooted Right Here

Miller's Bakery isn't just a place to grab something sweet — it's a piece of Johnson City itself. Our family has called this community home for generations, and when we opened our doors in 2018, we wanted to create something the neighborhood could be proud of: a real, from-scratch bakery built on genuine care and familiar flavors.

Growing up here, we remember what it felt like when a local business knew your name and your usual order. That's exactly the experience we set out to create. Every morning before sunrise, our family is in the kitchen — mixing dough, filling doughnuts, layering cheesecakes, and pulling fresh cookies from the oven — because we believe you can taste the difference when something is made with that kind of intention.

From our gourmet doughnuts to our gluten-free selections, everything on our menu is made from scratch using quality ingredients. Whether you grew up down the street or you're new to the area, consider this your neighborhood bakery.
