Our Story

A Little Bakery with a Big Heart

Miller's Bakery has been a beloved part of the Johnson City community since 2018. What started as a passion for handcrafted baking has grown into a neighborhood staple — a cozy spot where regulars know us by name and first-timers always come back.

Our small, dedicated team starts before sunrise each day, mixing dough, preparing fillings, and pulling trays fresh from the oven so that everything you pick up is as good as it gets. We believe food made with care just tastes better — and our neighbors agree.

Whether you're grabbing a sourdough loaf on your morning walk, celebrating a birthday with one of our custom cakes, or sneaking in for a seasonal pastry, you'll always find something made from scratch.
