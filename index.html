Our Story

A Neighborhood Bakery Built on Scratch-Made Goodness

Since 2018, Miller's Bakery has been a beloved corner of the Johnson City community. We believe the best baked goods start with simple, quality ingredients and the hands of people who genuinely care about what they make.

🌾

Made from scratch every morning. Our sourdough is mixed, proofed, and pulled from the oven while most people are still asleep.

🎂

Custom cakes for every occasion. Weddings, birthdays, anniversaries — we design cakes that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

❤️

A small team, a big heart. Every item that leaves our oven carries the love and attention of our dedicated bakers.
