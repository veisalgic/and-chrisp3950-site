Our Story

A Bakery Built on
Community & Craft

Miller's Bakery has been a warm corner of the Johnson City community since we first opened our doors in 2018. What started as a passion for homemade baking quickly grew into a beloved gathering spot where neighbors come for fresh bread and stay for the conversation.

Every single item that leaves our kitchen is made entirely from scratch. Our sourdough loaves are shaped and proofed overnight so they're ready first thing in the morning. Our custom cakes are built and decorated by hand, layer by layer, with care that shows in every slice.

Whether you pop in for a morning pastry and a coffee chat or you're trusting us with your wedding cake, our small team is dedicated to making sure every bite is worth it. We're more than a bakery — we're your neighbors, and we treat every order that way.

Special orders are always welcome with 48 hours' notice. Just call us or stop by — we'll take great care of you.
