Our Story

Baked with Passion, Shared with Community

Miller's Bakery opened in 2018 with one simple belief: people deserve real, honest food made with care. We're a small, tight-knit team working out of our cozy storefront at 142 Main Street — and every item on our shelves is a labor of love.

From our award-worthy sourdough loaves (fermented overnight, baked fresh each morning) to our custom wedding cakes and holiday pastries, we bring the same attention to detail whether we're making a birthday cake for 100 guests or a single croissant for your Tuesday morning.

We're proud to be part of the Johnson City community and we're grateful for every customer who walks through our door.

What We Make

Fresh Out of Our Oven

Whether you're stopping in for a morning treat or planning something special, we've got something wonderful waiting for you.

🍞

Sourdough Breads

Long-fermented, hand-shaped loaves baked fresh every morning. Classic sourdough, rosemary olive oil, and rotating seasonal varieties.

🎂

Custom Celebration Cakes

Birthdays, weddings, anniversaries — tell us your vision and we'll create something that tastes as beautiful as it looks. Order with 48 hours notice.

🥐

Seasonal Pastries

Croissants, danishes, scones, and muffins made with whatever's best in season. Follow us on social to see what's hot this week.

🍪

Cookies & Bars

Classic chocolate chip, snickerdoodle, lemon bars, and rotating seasonal creations. Perfect by the dozen for the office or a gathering.
