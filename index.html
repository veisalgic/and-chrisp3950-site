Our Story

Baking Happiness Since Day One

Miller's Bakery was born from a simple belief: that great bread and pastries don't need shortcuts. Every loaf, croissant, and celebration cake that leaves our kitchen is crafted by hand using recipes refined over years of passionate baking.

We source our flour from local mills, our eggs from nearby farms, and our fruits from seasonal growers — because what goes in truly matters. Whether you're picking up a morning sourdough or ordering a custom wedding cake, you'll taste the difference that real care makes.

Come visit us, and we'll prove it one bite at a time.

What We Bake

Our Most-Loved Creations

From crusty sourdoughs to flaky croissants and decadent celebration cakes, there's something for every craving at Miller's Bakery.

Sourdough Loaves

Long-fermented, naturally leavened with a crispy crust and chewy, open crumb. A bakery staple available every day.

Butter Croissants

72-hour laminated dough with European-style butter — impossibly flaky, golden, and deeply buttery in every layer.

Cinnamon Rolls

Soft, pillowy rolls filled with cinnamon-brown sugar butter and finished with a thick cream cheese glaze. Weekend mornings made perfect.

Celebration Cakes

Stunning custom cakes for any occasion — from intimate birthdays to grand wedding tiers. Designed and baked to your specifications.

Seasonal Fruit Tarts

Buttery shortcrust shells filled with silky pastry cream and topped with the freshest seasonal fruits sourced from local growers.

Multigrain & Rye Loaves

Hearty, seed-studded loaves packed with nutrition and flavor — ideal for sandwiches or served simply with good butter.
