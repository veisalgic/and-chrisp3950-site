Our Story

Baked with heart, served with a smile

Since 2018, Miller's Bakery has been a beloved part of the Johnson City community. Every loaf, pastry, and celebration cake that leaves our kitchen is made from scratch by a small, passionate team that genuinely cares about what ends up on your table.

Whether you're a regular stopping by for your morning sourdough or a couple planning their dream wedding cake, we treat every order — big or small — with the same love and attention. Come find us at 142 Main Street and smell what's coming out of the oven today.
