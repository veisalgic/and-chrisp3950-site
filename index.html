Our Story

A Community Staple Since 2018

Miller's Bakery was born out of a simple idea: that everyone deserves real, handcrafted food made with care. Since we opened our doors in Johnson City, we've been mixing, kneading, and baking from scratch every single morning — no shortcuts, no preservatives, just good honest baking.

Whether you're a regular stopping in for your Tuesday sourdough loaf or a newcomer ordering your first custom wedding cake, our small team treats every order like it's the most important thing in the oven. Because to us, it is.

What We Bake

Fresh From Our Oven

Every item is made from scratch using quality ingredients. From daily staples to once-in-a-lifetime celebration cakes, we pour love into every recipe.

🎂

Custom Celebration Cakes

Birthdays, weddings, anniversaries — our custom cakes are designed and baked just for your moment. Flavors, fillings, and decorations tailored to you. Order with 48 hours notice.

Freshly Baked Sourdough

Our sourdough is made from a long-fermented starter, shaped by hand, and baked in-house every single morning. Crusty on the outside, perfectly chewy inside — a customer favorite.
