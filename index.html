Our Story

A Neighborhood Bakery
Built on Real Ingredients

Miller's Bakery opened its doors in 2018 with one simple promise: bake everything from scratch, every day. No shortcuts, no mixes — just real flour, butter, and the kind of care you can taste.

Nestled at 142 Main Street in the heart of Johnson City, our little storefront has become a morning ritual for locals. You'll find our small, dedicated team at the ovens before sunrise, making sure the first loaves are ready when the doors open.

Whether you're grabbing a coffee and croissant on your way to work or planning a show-stopping wedding cake, we treat every order — big or small — with the same hands-on attention. That's not just our business, it's our culture.

What We Bake

Something for Every
Sweet (and Savory) Craving

🍰

Custom Celebration Cakes

Birthdays, anniversaries, baby showers, weddings — we design and bake custom cakes tailored to your vision and taste. Order with 48 hours notice or reach out to plan something truly special.

🍞

Sourdough & Artisan Breads

Our sourdough is slow-fermented overnight and baked every morning. With a crackly crust and open crumb, it's the loaf your kitchen has been waiting for. Available while supplies last.

🥐

Pastries & Seasonal Treats

Buttery croissants, fruit danishes, cinnamon rolls, scones, and seasonal specialties — follow us on Facebook and Instagram to see what's coming out of the oven this week.

🍪

Cookies & Boxed Goods

Need a dozen cookies for the office, a box of brownies for a gathering, or a gift for someone special? We've got you covered with freshly baked favorites ready to grab and go.

📋

Special Orders Welcome

Need something that's not on the menu? We love a good challenge. Give us 48 hours notice and we'll make it happen. Just call or stop by 142 Main Street and let us know what you have in mind.
