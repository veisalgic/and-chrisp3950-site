Our Story

Made From Scratch,
Made With Heart

Miller's Bakery opened its doors in 2018 with a simple promise: bake everything fresh, use real ingredients, and treat every customer like a neighbor. That promise hasn't changed.


Our small, passionate team starts early every morning — long before the storefront opens — so that when you walk through our door, the sourdough is warm, the pastry case is full, and the whole place smells like home. Whether it's a Tuesday morning coffee cake or the wedding cake of your dreams, we pour the same care into everything we bake.
